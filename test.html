Cancer Research UK was a key player in radiotherapy, which now benefits more than ____________ patients every year in the UK.

How To Play

Try pointing your phone at some of those squares you printed and cut out. Pretty cool huh? Four of these squares can be arranged to make a 3D model of a plastic mask worn during radiotherapy. Think of it like a puzzle - see if you can put the mask pieces together the right way. The square with the numbers on it is important too. It shows you the different patterns your other squares might make once you've completed the mask. Compare the shape you've made with these shapes to figure out the answer to this question!

3D image courtesy of The Science Museum
